The Florida Track Club is a non-profit group incorporated under the laws of the state of Florida. It is based in Gainesville, Florida, and sponsors events such as fun runs, social get-togethers, and races throughout the year. Membership is open to runners of all ages and abilities. The mission of the Florida Track Club is to build the local running community and act as a social network that supports runners of all ages and abilities.


The Florida Track Club is a member of the Road Runners Club Of America and the USA Track and Field Florida Association.
