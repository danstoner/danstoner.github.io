John Holmes Trail Run - 50K, 16M & 9M

Southeast's Oldest 50K Trail Run

Croom Park / Withlacoochee State Forest

OCTOBER 6, 2018

Field is limited to 200 Runners

 

Fantastic Course, Beautiful Shirts, Great Awards, Delicious Post Race Feast, Super Volunteers

 

 


The John Holmes Trail Run is enjoying its 27th running in 2018. Until 2006 it was simply called the Croom Trail 50K. In 2006 Race Director Jim Bodoh, a long time Croom Trail Runner, renamed the race in honor of founder and Croom Trail staple, John Holmes.
