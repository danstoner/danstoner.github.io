2018 BTA Hike 50 + 5 Challenge

2018 is the 50th Anniversary of the National Trails System Act, and Ohio is the first state to honor this moment by designating 2018 as Ohio’s Year of the Trails with Senate Concurrent Resolution 15! To celebrate both, we’re challenging ourselves to hike 50 miles on the BT and/or give back 5 hours of volunteer service to the trail we love.

Enjoy the BT! Give Back! Visit the Hike 50 + 5 page for all the details.

Tomfoolery Outdoors Follows the Blue Blazes!

Tom Helbig is an Ohio native, adventurer, motivational speaker and owner of Tomfoolery Outdoors. Tomfoolery Outdoors is dedicated to encouraging others to Live Active, Laugh More, Inspire All through adventure advocacy.

Tomfoolery started following the blue blazes around Ohio on the 1,400+ mile Buckeye Trail. He just completed the nations largest loop trail in September in the Road Fork and Whipple Sections. For more information about Tom's hike, where he's speaking, or to see what he was up to during his travel around the state, visit the Tomfoolery Outdoors Page.

Emma "Grandma" Gatewood Historical Marker

On May 28, 2016, the Ohio History Connection along with Trail Magic and author and relative of Grandma Gatewood, Ben Montgomery, erected a new historical marker dedicated to Emma "Grandma" Gatewood in the Cheshire Village Park in Cheshire, Ohio.

  • Here's a link to a local news article about the event: Galliopolis Daily Tribune
